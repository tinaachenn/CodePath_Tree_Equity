Getting Involved

Take Action

There are several ways for you to take action today to help your city become a lush urban forest. We can guide you in writing to congress about advancing tree planting bills, in writing to the EPA to demand more investment in tree related programs, in determining how to lower your energy bills at home, and even connecting you with organizations that work towards tree equity and run workshops that teach the basics of tree plantings.

Partner with American Forests

American Forests is the oldest national conservation organization in the United States. They have more than a century of experience championing the creation of policies and programs that address the forest-related challenges of the day. American Forests partners with people and entities at the local, state and federal levels who understand the critical role of forests in slowing climate change and advancing social equity. Partners share core values with American Forests and utilizes their resources and network to empower and provide people to take action.

Support American Forests

We understand that not everyone has the ability nor resources to physically take action. Thus, one last way to get involved is to support the mission of American Forests and contribute to the funds needed to continue old projects and intiate new ones. Your donation goes a long way and makes as much of an impact and our other methods of involvement. We hope you'll help us in our mission.
